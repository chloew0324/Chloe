About

I'm Chloe Wang, a human-computer interaction researcher and interdisciplinary designer. My practice blends interactive media, biosensing, and material exploration to study how people feel, remember, and relate across human and non-human worlds.

I prototype creative tools - from code-driven drawing systems and mixed-reality sketches to personalized fabrication workflows - that invite more fluid dialogues with machines.

Outside the lab, I'm also an illustrator, using algorithms, generative methods, and physical computing to drive illustration and art-making, turning technical processes into visual storytelling.
