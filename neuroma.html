Neuroma

Neuroma is a scented candle packaging concept shaped around soft color gradients, floral cues, and a calm emotional atmosphere.

The identity extends across boxes, vessel labels, tags, seals, and inserts to build a dreamy and gentle unboxing experience.

Scent Blotters

Three floral blotters extend the identity into a tactile scent sampling moment, letting the user preview each candle mood before opening the full package.

Cloud Nap

A cool lavender scent direction designed for quiet evenings and slow bedside rituals.

Floral Gradient

Soft petals, stone tones, and a calm floral blend create a gentle everyday mood.

Late Afternoon

Warm citrus and amber notes bring a brighter, sunlit finish to the collection.
